Introduction

Something unfamiliar to me, something strange to feel, what is my THE OTHER? I thought about things I couldn't understand to answer this question. The most incomprehensible of all was "infinity," which felt fundamentally different from me.

There is an end to my life. This is a truth that never changes. Can I understand the concept of 'endless' with a clear end? How do I feel about and accept the concept of infinity?

I would like to include in this article the results of my consideration of infinity, and of my limited self.
