On the other hand, what about me? My life will end in death one day. I am obviously a finite being, and that is why I am fundamentally different from infinity.

Because my life is finite, all I can do, where I can go, and what I can experience is finite. It's as if I'm tied to a chain called life. The line is so long that I seem to be walking around freely, but in fact there are clear limits set prior to my existence.

For example, as of 2017, Korean women's life expectancy is about 86 years. I can probably live another 60 years or so from now on, and if I'm lucky enough to live a long life, I can live another 20 years. There are about 200 countries in the world, so if I try, I might be able to look around the world before I die. That would be an amazing achievement, but if I’m targeting the whole universe, not the whole world, it's impossible without having to calculate. My life span will not allow that.
